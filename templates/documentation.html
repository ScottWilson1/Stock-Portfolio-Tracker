FAQ

    How frequently are prices updated?
    Prices updates are rate limited by Yahoo Finance and update in the yFinance python library every 15 minutes.

    How many stocks can I track?
    Users are welcome to track up to 10 stocks while in the free membership tier. Additional features can be unlocked with a premium subscription.
